About Us

Since 1989... oldest and trusted brand

Krishna Enterprise is a company engaged in the manufacturing of magnetic door catchers under the brand name of "MEESHA". Krishna Enterprise has been churning out magnetic door catchers since year 1989, which have manufacturing facilities at Ahmedabad,(Gujarat-India) and their products are well established in the market.

Company's Vision

The company has always been maintaining a pacesetter for the future times. Companies main concentration is totally on the development of support quality product for the international and indian markets. The company is also maintaining a total quality management.

Research & Development

Research of new types of magnetic door catchers and development of new products related to magnetic products, KRISHNA Enterprise is the fundamental part of this activity. The company is engaged in continuous research and experiment on other type of other high exclusive magnetic door catcher to obtain productive results with improved characteristics, which once perfected, replace the existing product. The advance drive and more efficient technology together results in lower energy consumption and reduced process time. With constant innovation, it is creating its own benchmarks.

Marketing, Distribution & Service Network

Marketing strategies are also conducted from time to time. Companies have also appointed dealers and distributors at all levels who are regularly handling the marketing and distribution network of products manufactured by the company. Methodology, management & consistency of process provide the environment for achieving perfection. We go far beyond measurement of components, its commitment to achieving perfection in all aspects of manufacturing and is reflected in our customer satisfaction with our products.
